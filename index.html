Unwind

2016
Book
4K Text

Frey is held captive by the most powerful man in Shreve -- her father. Frey, who is Rafi’s body double, was cloned to keep Rafi out of any possible danger. The twins switched-up on their father who now thinks Frey is Rafi and is now hunting down his actual daughter. Infact, no one knows who Frey really is: a strategist fighter who’s trained all her life to protect her sister Rafi. Frey who eventually escapes her father forms a rebellion to take down her creator and ends up stranded in the city of Paz, a place where its citizens control their emotions through a panel of buttons on their wrists. Paz undergoes a tragic attack and Frey must escape once again and find her lost sister.
